About Us

Falco company, based in Prishtina, Kosova, has been in operation since 2004. It is a privately-owned business that specializes in heavy lifting services and specialized transport. Falco has gained a reputation for providing reliable and efficient solutions to meet the needs of its clients. The primary focus of Falco is on heavy lifting services. They have a range of advanced machinery at their disposal, including autocranes with lifting capacities ranging from 45 tons to 200 tons. Additionally, they have equipment such as excavators, front loaders, crushers, and more. These tools enable Falco to efficiently handle complex lifting tasks. One key aspect that sets Falco apart is their team of skilled operators. These operators have years of experience in executing challenging lifting tasks safely and efficiently. They are well-equipped to handle various projects, ensuring that they are completed on time and meet the highest standards. Overall, Falco Company is a leading provider of heavy lifting services and specialized transport in Prishtina, Kosova. With their state-of-the-art machinery and experienced operators, they are capable of delivering reliable and efficient solutions to their clients' needs.
